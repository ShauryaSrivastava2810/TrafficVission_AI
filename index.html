Traffic Pattern Prediction Project

AI Model: Advanced Virtual Traffic Intelligence (AVTI)

Serial Number: AVTI-TR3FF1C

In recent years, the rapid growth of urban areas has led to increased traffic congestion, necessitating the development of advanced solutions to monitor and manage traffic flow efficiently. Traditional methods of traffic monitoring, such as manual counting and fixed sensors, are often limited in scope and accuracy. To address these challenges, this project leverages deep learning techniques to analyze traffic patterns from visual data, providing a more dynamic and accurate assessment of traffic conditions. The primary goal of this project is to create a robust and efficient system that can classify and predict traffic patterns from both images and videos. By employing state-of-the-art deep learning models, we aim to provide real-time insights into traffic conditions, which can be utilized by urban planners, traffic management authorities, and autonomous vehicles.
